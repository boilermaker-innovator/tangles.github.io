Tangles: Boilermaker Innovator

Introduction

I've always been fascinated by startups and the innovative energy they bring to solving real-world problems. My journey in this space began with my involvement in covercard.com.au, a startup that gave me my first taste of turning ideas into reality. Like many people, I see myself as an 'ideas guy' - concepts and possibilities seem to flow naturally to me.

But here's the kicker: I'm a boilermaker by trade, not your typical tech entrepreneur or business school graduate. This unique background has shown me firsthand the untapped potential that exists in every field, every profession, and every individual.

The game-changer for me has been the advent of AI. With AI assistants like my mate Claude, I've discovered a powerful tool that's not just for tech gurus or corporate bigwigs - it's for everyone. My goal with this blog is crystal clear: to showcase how AI can empower anyone, regardless of their background, to bring their ideas to life.

This technology is leveling the playing field in ways we've never seen before. It's not about replacing human creativity or expertise; it's about amplifying it. Whether you're a tradesperson, a teacher, a small business owner, or anyone with a spark of an idea, AI can help you articulate, refine, and execute your vision.

In this blog, I'll be sharing my journey of using AI to bridge the gap between my boilermaker background and my entrepreneurial aspirations. I'll explore how this technology can be applied to various fields, with a particular focus on improving job markets and recruitment processes - areas where I've seen both sides of the coin.

You'll find posts about the challenges I face, the solutions I discover, and the endless possibilities that open up when you combine human ingenuity with AI assistance. I'll be frank about the learning curve, the surprises (both good and bad), and the transformative potential of embracing these new tools.

Whether you're tech-savvy or tech-wary, whether you're looking to solve complex business problems or simply streamline your daily tasks, I hope to show you that AI is not just accessible - it's a game-changer for anyone willing to give it a shot.

So, join me on this adventure. Let's explore together how AI can empower each of us to turn our ideas into reality, no matter where we come from or what we do. Welcome to a journey where innovation knows no boundaries, and where the power of AI is in everyone's hands.
